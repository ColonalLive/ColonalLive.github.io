Colonal Mod Pack FAQ

What is Colonal Pack?

The Colonal Mod Pack (CMP) is a collection of mods assembled by Colonal. It was designed to play Hardcore Minecraft with a "French Vanilla" feeling. Meaning there are only performance-enhancing and quality-of-life mods, but doesn't alter Vanilla gameplay mechanics. This balances the ability to have a large Redstone contraption and have still enough FPS and tools for high-quality content creation.

What resource packs, mods do you use?

Check out the Packs page to see all this info.
Note, you can see the indivigual mods used in the Colonal Mod Pack can be found on the "relations" tab in cursforge. 

Why do some resource packs causes issues with chests?

The Fast Chest mod replaces all chest entities with solid blocks, which greatly improves performance in storage rooms with lots of chests. But ths changes how the texture is loaded and is not compatible with most resource packs.

What is the colorful background/shapes visible during some projects?

MiniHud/Litematica are the mods, it has the ability to overlay transparent shapes into the world to help guide Colonal during massive builds.

What do I press to use Mini Hud?

The main keybind has been changed to numpad 7 for all Minihud key combinations.

What do I press to use litematica?

The main keybind has been changed to numpad 9 for all litematica key combinations.
